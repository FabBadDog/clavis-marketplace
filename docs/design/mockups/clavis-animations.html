Conversation stream
The highest-traffic surface. Turns and tool rows arrive with a fade + slide-up; tool bursts stagger; the streaming status crossfades into the rendered response. Living indicators mark active work.

standard · ease-out · staggered

Docking surface
Today the whole tree re-renders instantly on every dock / close / split. The biggest gap. Tiles grow in and collapse out, tab content crossfades, and the drop-hint glides between zones as the cursor moves.

standard · ease-out

Lists & selection
The defining list pattern across palette, events, git-log, keymap. Selection animates — the 1.5px left border and 2.5% tint glide to the new row. Hover fades on every row. Filtering reflows with accent-highlighted matches. Drill-down slides.
