How to read it

Dot to the upper-left of the diagonal → spent less than the time gone → surplus.

Dot to the lower-right → spent more than the time gone → overspend.

The dot keeps its window colour (blue = 5-hour, gold = weekly); the sign lives entirely in the red/green surplus / overspend wording.

The runway below is in real tokens: the dashed line is the first ceiling you hit — here the weekly window at 100k, even though the 5-hour slot still shows 200k.
